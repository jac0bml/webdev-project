Lynk The Lion

Lynk the Lion was built out of recycled electronics and is on display at my high school. After seeing lots of electronics and other related items be considered too obsolete, the concept was born of displaying different pieces of technology in a way which allows people to see it in a new light. This encourages repurposing and reusing before recycling or throwing away.

Eventually, an old functional laptop was placed below the lion which would periodically (or on command) play different lion sounds.
